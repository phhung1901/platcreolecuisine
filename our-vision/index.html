    About PlatCreole

    We are a family-owned Haitian restaurant focusing on providing a glimpse of the Haitian Culture's cuisine no matter the background or ethnicity. We do not have place for discrimination nor social racism. We simply believe in the power of people and only they can give us the power we need to move forward.


    PlatCreole strive to set itself apart from the common stigma of Haitian restaurants by combining culinary delights with exceptional customer service to create a unique experience every time you visit us. We strive to make each visit satisfying and memorable.  Above all, we want you to feel at home.
